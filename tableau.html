Netflix Dataset

Interactive dashboard collection utilizing Netflix datasets, designed to provide key stakeholders on the trend of company stock price and factors influencing its fluctuations, along with general data. Each page focuses on specific metrics, KPIs or area of interest, creating a cohesive set of visualizations for decision-makers to navigate. The story board provides an analysis on the stock price, accompanied by strategic recommendations on factors to prioritize and relevant metrics to track to foster growth of the company.
